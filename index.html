Carpentry

I've been absolutely smitten with the sweet scent of freshly cut wood. But my foray into the world of carpentry is a bit like the plot twist in a Hollywood movie - it didn't happen until a few years back.

I'm a self-taught wood whisperer with an undying reverence for the raw, unadulterated beauty of timber. I relish the moments when I can kick back without any pressure, unleashing my inner carpentry ideas. First, there's the thrill of sketching out a project on a piece of paper, and then it's time for the grand finale - calculating the measurements. But let me tell you, that's just the warm-up act. The real fun begins when something inevitably goes awry, and you're suddenly thrown into a whirlwind of alternative solutions. And you know what? I absolutely thrive on it!
