#about

How does it start? One fine day, I was scribbling down my carpentry workshop's inventory on a random piece of paper, and then it hit me - I need a program for this madness! And so, I decided to embark on the adventure of coding my very own ERP system. And it wasn't a walk in the park, not even a tiny stroll!

So, I took baby steps, almost like I was learning to crawl in the world of coding. I found myself diving into a sea of books, watching tutorials that felt like they were explaining quantum physics, and even tuning into coding podcasts that spoke in some secret language... I was trying to tackle more challenging ideas, and gradually, coding became more and more fun.

Back when I was young (which feels like eons ago), I was a bit like a lost puppy in a maze of future career plans. I had no idea who I wanted to be when I'll grow up. I searched high and low, but eventually, I stumbled upon something that truly lit a fire in me.

I mean, sure, I could've been a logistician, an architect, or even a hipster artist sipping soy lattes at a trendy cafe, but as a programmer, I discovered I could work in any field. It's a flexible world where you can gain knowledge in just about any category. Now, my dream is crystal clear, and one day, mark my words, I'll be a Fullstack Developer!
